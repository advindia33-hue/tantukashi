The Art of Banarasi Weaving

Banarasi silk weaving is one of India's most celebrated textile traditions. For centuries artisans in Varanasi have created luxurious silk sarees using intricate zari weaving techniques passed through generations. Each saree takes days or even weeks to complete. Every motif, thread and pattern reflects the rich cultural heritage of Banaras. At TantuKashi we bring these authentic handwoven sarees directly from the looms of Varanasi to modern wardrobes.
