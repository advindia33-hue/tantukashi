The Art of Banarasi Weaving

A Pashmina Banarasi silk saree is a refined fusion of Himalayan Pashmina wool and the heritage weaving traditions of Varanasi. Unlike traditional matte Pashmina, this blend uses a silk warp, giving the saree a graceful drape with a subtle, luxurious sheen. These sarees are known for intricate Banarasi motifs like Butidar and Jal, woven with fine zari. The result is a premium textile that combines warmth, durability, and ornate craftsmanship—ideal for elegant winter occasions.
